    The beautiful thing about drawing is there are really no requirements or expectations. You can have just a piece of paper and paper to begin.

    I prefer pencil drawings to painting or other mediums because I can change or adapt the drawing however and whenever (at any point in the creative process) I like.

    Listening to music while drawing has a meditating effect for me.
